Spencer Nelson’s Personal Finances

Personal project – not offered to the public

What this is: a private workflow I built to view my account balances, transactions, and investments in one place. It uses Plaid to connect to my own financial institutions, and n8n to automate data pulls and personal notifications.

Key points

  • This app is for my personal use only. No signup, no third-party users.
  • I do not sell, share, or monetize any data.
  • Connections are handled via Plaid. Tokens are stored privately in my n8n instance.
